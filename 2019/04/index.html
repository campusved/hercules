Welcome to Herculus Law Consultants

Herculus Law Consultants (ALC) is a multi-disciplinary, multi-locational Law Firm having a team of experienced partners and associates who are Advocates, Company Secretaries, Management Consultants, Chartered Accountants having average experience bandwidth of around 15 years. The Firm is having Partners and Associates who have extensive professional experience in wide spectrum of services viz. Licencing & Registration, Compliance Management, Corporate Restructuring, Corporate and General Law Litigation Matters, Arbitration and Alternate Dispute Resolution, Merger & Amalgamation, Liasioning and Representation, etc.

The Firm with the team of experienced professionals is having full service capability to handhold the clients at various stages of business requirements with utmost sincerity, dedication and trust.

Presently, the firm is having offices at Delhi, Noida, Kolkata and Patna with associate network of PAN India.
